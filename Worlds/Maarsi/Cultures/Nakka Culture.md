The **Nakka Culture** is one of the oldest known cultures of [[Maarsi]]. While its influence over history as a whole was minimal, it dominated much of the western continent for the majority of the Stone Age and left a heavy impact on the culture customs of the [[Astiga Culture|Astiga]] culture.
### Settlements
